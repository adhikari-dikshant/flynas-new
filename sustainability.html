Our Sustainability Commitment

We’re not just flying — we’re flying responsibly. From reducing carbon emissions and modernizing our fleet, to empowering communities and going digital for good — sustainability is at the heart of our journey.

Fuel Efficiency

Our modern fleet of Airbus A320 and A330 aircraft are among the most fuel-efficient in the industry, reducing our carbon footprint with every flight.

Carbon Reduction

We're committed to reducing our carbon emissions by 20% by 2030 through operational improvements, technology investments, and sustainable aviation fuel.

Reduced Waste

Our cabin service is designed to minimize waste, with recycling programs for plastics, paper, and aluminum. We're constantly innovating to reduce single-use plastics.
